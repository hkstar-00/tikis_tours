About Us

Who we are

Tiki's Tours is new eco-tourism venture whose mission is to showcase the remarkable natural attractions of the Waitomo. The wide range of scenic tour options avaiable through scenic tours, heritage tours as well as guided history tours, which includes stunning karst landscapes, waterfalls, scenic walkways, the popular glow worn caves and working farms, numerous other limestone caves are found throughout the Waitomo district. All our tours cover green areas like Te Kuiti, Waimiha and Pureora.

Our Mission

  • Provide authentic New Zealand experiences and informative tours to domestic and international visitors.

  • Be innovative and cost effective.

  • To provide you the best experiences through out tours.
